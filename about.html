Our Foundation

Decades of Industrial Leadership

Founded on the principles of reliability and logistical mastery, PT Jaya Bersama Terang has evolved from a regional distributor into a world-class FMCG powerhouse. Our journey is marked by a relentless pursuit of culinary excellence and supply chain efficiency.

Today, we stand at the intersection of traditional quality and modern innovation, operating state-of-the-art facilities that serve international retail partners across multiple continents. Every product that leaves our facility carries our seal of unwavering integrity.
